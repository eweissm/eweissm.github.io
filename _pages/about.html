What Do I Research?

My research aims to make four-legged robots move more like animals. Today’s quadruped robots are fast but rigid, limiting their agility and efficiency, especially in tight, rough, or aquatic environments. Inspired by animals like dogs and otters, I am developing robots with flexible, compliant spines and artificial muscles. By combining advanced modeling, innovative hardware design, novel soft actuators, and AI-based control, I am seeking to understand how spine flexibility improves speed, energy efficiency, stability, and maneuverability on land and in water.
